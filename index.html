Biography

I am a Lecturer in Computational Social Science in the Department of Sociology at the University of Essex. My research leverages large datasets and methods from computational social science, social data science, and network analysis to study diverse social phenomena such as transparency and reproducibility of scientific research, human mobility, global migration, inequality, and digital-health interventions.
